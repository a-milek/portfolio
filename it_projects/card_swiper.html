Card Swiper

Purpose: Automatic testing of a card reader device

A short and simple electronics excercise that I did. I have successfully integrated a Raspberry Pi, PCA9685-based servo HAT, and an RC Servo to develop a card swiper system. The program is accessible through SSH, allowing external users to utilize the system. The goal for it was to be mobile so the RC Servo is connected to metal plates to weigh it down and keep it stable.
