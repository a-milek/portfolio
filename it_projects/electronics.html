Electronics activities

I have moderate experience with soldering and electronics, but I have found that these individual projects are not large enough to warrant separate pages for each and every one. Therefore, I have chosen to group them for convenience.
Click on the pictures for a zoommed-in version!

Pocket size Voltmeter

My father required a compact volt meter for measuring the voltage of batteries while on the move. This particular meter is equipped with a T-Dean plug for easy connection. I assembled it through soldering and encased it in a thermo-reactive casing and hot glue to improve its durability and strength.

Alarm clock

My first soldering project involved assembling an alarm clock to test out my soldering skills. The project proceeded smoothly without any complications. It made me appreciate the soldering process and made me more confident in the electronics field!



Inductor

I acquired a valuable skill by constructing an inductor using a metal core and insulated wire.
